RECO

Mandatory: The RECO Information Guide

This is not optional. Under Ontario's Trust in Real Estate Services Act, 2002 (TRESA), every real estate agent in Ontario must provide consumers with the RECO Information Guide before providing any services or signing any agreement. If you are buying or selling real estate in Ontario, this is the first document you should read. It explains your rights as a client, what a registered brokerage owes you, and the risks of proceeding without professional representation.
