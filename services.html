Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

New to buying or selling in Ottawa? Here are the questions we get asked most — answered honestly.

Ask Us Anything
Do I need a realtor to buy a home?

Technically no — but working with a realtor costs you nothing as a buyer (the seller pays the commission) and gives you market insight, negotiation expertise, and someone in your corner through the entire process.

How long does it take to buy a home in Ottawa?

From initial search to closing, the typical Ottawa purchase takes 60–90 days — though it can be faster or slower depending on your financing, how competitive the market is, and the property type.

What neighbourhoods do you cover?

We serve all of Ottawa and the surrounding communities — including Westboro, Barrhaven, Kanata, Nepean, Orleans, Glebe, Stittsville, Manotick, and Riverside South.

Is now a good time to invest in Ottawa real estate?

Ottawa's market is underpinned by government employment stability and growing tech demand in Kanata. For long-term investors, Ottawa continues to offer solid fundamentals. We can walk you through current market conditions in a free consultation.

What makes Accliv different from other agents?

We're new to the industry — which means no divided attention, no old habits, and no coasting on past deals. Every client gets our full focus, deep preparation, and a genuine commitment to their outcome.
