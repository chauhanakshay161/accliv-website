Residential Investment

Ottawa's Rental Market Has a Logic. Learn It Before You Buy.

Kanata North's rental demand runs on tech employment. 30,000 workers at 500 companies who need somewhere to live. Orleans draws bilingual families who stay long-term. Riverside South is a pre-LRT-completion story, and those windows close once the station opens. Each corridor has its own demand driver, its own vacancy profile, its own hold horizon.

Before any income property acquisition, our team models cash flow, cap rate, vacancy risk, and exit options under multiple scenarios. If the numbers do not hold under pressure, the honest answer is no.

Commercial Real Estate

Where Your Business Operates Matters as Much as Where You Live.

Ottawa's commercial market has its own logic. Kanata North's office corridors fill on tech cycles. Bank Street retail performs on density and foot traffic, not just location. The Sparks and Wellington corridors carry government-adjacent premiums. Industrial land in Gloucester and Nepean operates on entirely different fundamentals than anything residential.

Commercial acquisitions demand a different level of due diligence: environmental assessments, lease covenant reviews, CAM charge analysis, zoning confirmation, and financing structures that residential lenders won't touch. Our team approaches commercial files the same way we approach residential ones, with the full picture built before any commitment is made, and with direct connections to the legal, inspection, and financing specialists that commercial transactions require.

  • Office and retail property acquisition and disposition
  • Industrial and mixed-use property
  • Commercial investment: cap rate and NOI analysis
  • Multi-tenant income property sales
  • Commercial lease review and negotiation support
  • Due diligence coordination and specialist referrals

Ask Us Anything
Can you help with commercial real estate, not just homes?

Yes. Accliv Group handles residential, commercial, and investment real estate across Ottawa. Commercial files require a different level of due diligence: environmental assessments, lease reviews, CAM charge analysis, commercial financing structures. Our team approaches them with the same disciplined process as residential transactions, supported by a network of commercial specialists including real estate lawyers, AACI appraisers, and environmental consultants.

What does Ottawa's commercial market look like right now?

Ottawa's commercial market is segmented and uneven. Kanata North office absorption follows tech employment cycles. When hiring slows, vacancy rises. Bank Street and Orleans retail corridors perform on population density. Industrial space in Gloucester and Nepean remains consistently low vacancy due to limited new supply. The government-adjacent office corridors around Sparks Street carry premium rental rates on long-term federal tenancies. Each segment operates by different fundamentals and requires market-specific analysis before any capital is committed.

How long does an Ottawa property purchase take, start to finish?

Residential purchases typically close in 60–90 days from the accepted offer. Commercial transactions take longer, typically 90 to 180 days, due to environmental assessments, financing approvals, and conditional period requirements. The search and negotiation phase varies significantly by property type and how competitive the specific market segment is when you are buying.

What professional connections do you bring to a transaction?

Behind every Accliv file is a set of pre-vetted professional relationships built specifically for Ottawa real estate: real estate lawyers for both residential and commercial closings, mortgage brokers with access to residential and commercial lenders, property inspectors for residential condition assessments, AACI appraisers for complex valuations, property managers for investors, environmental consultants for commercial due diligence, and contractors for renovation cost assessments. These are direct introductions, not referral lists.

Why work with Accliv instead of a larger team or brokerage?

Larger teams spread attention across more files. With Accliv Group, our experienced team covers every engagement completely: the research, the strategy, the negotiation, the execution. The analysis is built from the data up, not from instinct. The professional network means the specialists required for complex transactions are ready to move when needed. The result is tighter transactions, fewer surprises, and a coordinated team managing every aspect from first call to closing.
