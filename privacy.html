Overview

Accliv Group ("we," "us," or "our") operates acclivgroup.com and is committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who visits this website or contacts us. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under Canadian privacy law, including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).

By using this website or submitting any form, you acknowledge the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We only collect information you choose to provide to us directly. This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or behavioural advertising. We collect:

Contact Form Submissions

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • The service you are interested in
  • Your message content

Listing Notification Sign-Up

  • Email address

Automatic Technical Data

Like all websites, our server logs may record your IP address and browser type for security and performance purposes. This data is not used for marketing, is not linked to your identity, and is retained for a maximum of 30 days.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide exclusively to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or service request
  • Provide the real estate information or consultation you requested
  • Notify you of new property listings, if you have opted in
  • Fulfill our legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, share, or trade your personal information with any third party for their marketing purposes. Your information will not be added to any mailing list without your explicit consent, as required by CASL.

CASL & Email Communications

Contact Form: Submitting our contact form constitutes implied consent under CASL for us to respond to your specific inquiry. It does not enroll you in any ongoing marketing communications.

Listing Notifications: Entering your email to receive listing notifications constitutes express consent under CASL for that specific purpose. You may unsubscribe from these notifications at any time by replying to any notification email with the word "UNSUBSCRIBE" or by contacting us directly at info@acclivgroup.com.

Every commercial electronic message we send will identify Accliv Group as the sender, include our contact information, and include a clear and easy mechanism to unsubscribe. We will process unsubscribe requests within 10 business days as required by CASL.

Third-Party Services

Google Fonts

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). When your browser requests these fonts, your IP address is transmitted to Google's servers. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for how Google uses this data.

Unsplash

Property and lifestyle images on this site are loaded from Unsplash (images.unsplash.com). Accessing these images may transmit your IP address to Unsplash's servers.

GitHub Pages

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical data related to your visit in accordance with GitHub's Privacy Statement.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party tracking or advertising tools on this website.

Data Retention

We retain personal information submitted through our contact form for as long as necessary to complete your inquiry and any resulting business relationship, or as required by law. If no business relationship develops, inquiry data is deleted after 24 months.

Listing notification email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

You may request deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at info@acclivgroup.com.

Your Rights

Under PIPEDA and applicable provincial privacy legislation, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent to the use of your personal information at any time (subject to legal or contractual restrictions)
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@acclivgroup.com with the subject line "Privacy Request." We will respond within 30 days.
